What is Cloud Computing?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eZLcyTxi8ZI
- A third party (AWS, Azure) take over and look after a companies data and computer processing over the internet
- Cloud is a utility that can be used for scalable, managed infrastructure
- You will only pay for what you use from a cloud provider
- It can grow and shrink as demands rise or fall
- Cloud providers supply servers, storage and networking that customers do not need to manage
- Compliance and regulations are followed to protect data
- There are different levels of resource management cloud providers offer - 
	- Iaas (Infrastructure as a Service) - high level of customer management
	- PaaS (Platform as a Service) - mid level of customer management
	- SaaS (Software as a Service) - very low level of customer management
